Karaage

Karaage is fried chicken, but not floating in grease and with delicious toppings!

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Mirin
  • Oyster sauce
  • Curry powder
  • Egg yolk
  • Japanese mayonaise
  • Flour
  • Potato starch
  • Sunflower oil
  1. Mix the garlic, sesame oil, mirin, oyster sauce, curry powder and egg yolk together. Cut up the chicken and leave to marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Lightly coat the chicken, shaking off excess flour so as not to cloud the oil.
  3. Fry twice, first at a lower temperature, then again on a higher heat to crisp.
